Amy Shark background

Amy Shark's real name is Amy Billings. Billings was born in 1986 on Australia's Gold Coast and attended Southport State High School. Although Amy Shark had been creating music content on YouTube for a while, in 2016 she shot to fame with the release of her first songs "Golden Fleece", which won Pop Song of the Year at the Queensland Music Awards, and "Adore". The Amy Shark song "Adore" received considerable airplay and subsequently led to a bidding war as Australia's major record labels raced to sign the talented young singer. In the end, Shark signed with Sony Music Australia in November 2016. 2017 saw the release of the debut Amy Shark EP Night Thinker, featuring songs like "Adore", "Drive You Mad", and  "Worst Girl", the EP showcased Amy Shark's talents as a thoughtful songwriter and shot to the Top 10 on iTunes in 15 countries. 2017 would get even better for Amy Shark as she was nominated for numerous awards and won an APRA award for Pop Work of the Year and two ARIA awards for Best Pop Release and Breakthrough Artist. So, to say this is a star on the rise would be a huge understatement, Amy Shark tickets are constantly in high demand so get yours today and avoid the disappointment of finding out that the Amy Shark concert has sold out.  

Amy Shark new album

Set for release on 13 July, the new Amy Shark album will be called Love Monster. Shark has already released the first single from her new album; "I Said Hi" was premiered on influential Australian radio station Triple J on 11 April, 2018 and released the very next day.
