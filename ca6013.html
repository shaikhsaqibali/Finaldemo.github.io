The AFL Season

Rich in tradition, the AFL is one of the oldest and most popular sport leagues played in Australia. Founded in 1897, the competition consists of eighteen teams spread over five states including Western Australia. Big names Carlton, Collingwood, Essendon, Geelong, Hawthorn, Richmond, West Coast Eagles and Western Bulldogs regularly contest fierce rivalries. So if you’re a fan of any of these mighty teams, support your team properly and buy your AFL Premiership tickets now! 

The AFL season begins with the NAB Cup, a pre-season competition, which is then followed by 23 home and away fixtures that run during the winter. The top eight teams then proceed to play in a finals series, which culminates in that all-important AFL Grand Final. The winners of that all-important match are crowned “Premiers” and awarded the premiership cup.
