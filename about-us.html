Guaranteed delivery without leaving your home.

We make sure that all tickets on our website arrive in time for the event.

Once the purchase has been confirmed, the seller will send the tickets to the address that the buyer has indicated.

In the case of last minute purchases, the tickets will be delivered to a pre-arranged location close to the venue to avoid unexpected problems and to ensure that the tickets will get to the buyer in time.

Why are there different prices?

The sellers list their tickets on our website and set the sale price they consider appropriate.

Not all tickets cost the same because each seller can set a different amount that may not correspond with the original ticket price. Due to this you can find tickets listed at above and below the original ticket price on StubHub.

How to choose the best ticket?

The sellers are responsible for sharing ticket details, in particular the exact location.

Choose tickets with the most information, such as block and row. This way you will be able to use the map to find your preferred location.

For events outside your city you can request for tickets to be delivered to your accommodation, such as a hotel or an apartment.
